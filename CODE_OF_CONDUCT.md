# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or professional role.

Be constructive, discuss evidence and tradeoffs, respect privacy, and assume
that questions are asked in good faith. Harassment, intimidation, personal
attacks, discriminatory language, and publication of another person's private
information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens the community or
the safety of research data.

Report conduct concerns through a private maintainer channel. Do not include
patient data or confidential institutional information in a report. Maintainers
will limit disclosure to what is necessary to review and resolve the concern.
